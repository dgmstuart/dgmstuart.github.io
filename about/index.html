About

Hello, I’m Duncan, and I can talk to both people and computers.

I’m the Technical Architect at dxw. That title means different things at different companies, but at dxw it means I spend most of my time in Vim writing code, and the rest of my time time talking to customers and end users about what we should be building.

I’ve been involved in making software and websites for a while. You can find out about that on LinkedIn or check out my shiny responsive CV.

You’ll can find me on other social networks too, like twitter, and instagram.

In my spare time I’m mostly swing dancing and rebuilding Swing Out London.

I’m very interested in the whole teaching kids to code thing. I volunteer some Saturdays at Wizzie Wizzie Computer Coding Club and I run a meetup for People teaching kids to code in London.
